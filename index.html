Press a number to start. Then select an operator, then another number, and so on. You must alternate between numbers and operators. The calculator remembers the last result and applies the operator to a new number.

The last result and whatever you selected should always be displayed.

The history list is updated every time you choose something. It displays a string of all your commands, followed by the result so far.
